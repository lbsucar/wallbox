Activity8
=========

Through a series of interfaces, the necessary objects that have acceptance methods are arranged.
These will receive objects, which can be extended by polymorphisms.
These accept methods control whether new visiting objects are accepted and will work with them accordingly.

Working with patterns should be mandatory. It allow to align the solutions developed by a team. 
And also creates moore value to the product from the point of view from the architecture perspective.
Also it is very helpful for the QA and DevOps professionals so they can start developing their solutions.
